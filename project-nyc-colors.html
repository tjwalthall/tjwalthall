OVERVIEW

New York City Colors is a photography project capturing the essence of Manhattan's neighborhoods through close-up color and texture. I photographed details of buildings and streets across different areas, then built a microsite where visitors could click through each neighborhood — from the Financial District to the East Village to Tribeca — and get a feel for its character before ever setting foot there.
