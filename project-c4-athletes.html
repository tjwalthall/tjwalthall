OVERVIEW

C4 had long been embraced by serious gym-goers and performance athletes, but as the brand continued to grow, we saw an opportunity to broaden its definition of who energy is for. We built a new brand platform around a simple belief: life itself is athletic.

Whether you’re chasing a championship, fighting fires, working a demanding job, or simply showing up every day, the things that matter require energy.
