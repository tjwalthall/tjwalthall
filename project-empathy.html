OVERVIEW

While Indeed’s core mission is to help people get jobs, we recognized that the hiring ecosystem is fundamentally broken for the 30 million people in the U.S. facing significant employment barriers. Empathy At Work was developed as a call to action to move from intent to impact.

I led the creative strategy for this initiative, moving beyond broad advocacy to inspire meaningful, tangible changes within the hiring process. This project wasn’t just about awareness; it was about equipping organizations with the simple, actionable steps necessary to build more inclusive, equitable communities.
