TJ is a veteran Creative Director with over 18 years of experience building, scaling, and leading high-performance creative teams across both elite global ad agencies and world-class in-house brand studios.

His career layout bridges both sides of the industry. Having directed multi-layered creative systems for heavy-network agencies globally, he transitioned into leadership roles within top-tier corporate operations—directing global creative infrastructure at Indeed and driving disruptive, high-velocity commercial pipelines for Nutrabolt / C4 Energy.

Operating at the intersection of technological innovation, filmic narrative, and scaled brand architecture, his oversight has consistently generated massive cultural resonance alongside verified business results. His work has earned the industry’s highest validation, including accolades at Cannes Lions (Gold), The One Show (Silver, Bronze, Merits), Webby Awards (Multiple Winner for Best Game & Technical Achievement), Effies (Gold), and the FWA.

Whether architecting global modern campaigns from within an organization or steering high-stakes creative transformation as an independent consultant, he focuses strictly on top-level creative leadership that scales.
