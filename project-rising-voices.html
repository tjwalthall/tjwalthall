OVERVIEW

Rising Voices was born from a provocative question: What would happen if, instead of spending a national TV budget on a single spot, we invested those resources directly into 10 BIPOC creators to help launch their careers?

The program was Indeed’s mission brought to life, launching over 50 director careers in Hollywood and creating over 2000 jobs from the film productions alone.
