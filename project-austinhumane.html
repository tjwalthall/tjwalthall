OVERVIEW

Retargeting banner ads are often viewed as the most intrusive form of digital advertising. We flipped this narrative by dramatizing the concept of a dog’s unconditional loyalty. The campaign used the persistent nature of retargeting ads to showcase shelter dogs that would follow potential owners.

The project turned a disliked medium into a highly effective bridge for adoption and was honored with the "People’s Choice" award at The Webby Awards.
