OVERVIEW

The Bad Breathalyzer was an ambitious experiment in interactive product sampling for Icebreakers Cool Blasts. Developed in secret with the team at Arnold Boston’s Innovation Labs, the machine was a functional prototype equipped with specialized sensors to quantify the chemical markers of bad breath—turning your morning coffee or lunch choices into a real-time performance metric. The worse the data, the more Icebreakers were dispensed.

While the machine was fully realized and technically sound, it ultimately never saw the fluorescent light of a real-world bar. It remains one of my favorite projects.
