OVERVIEW

Volvo Reality is the first virtual reality test drive on your smartphone.

Our team at R/GA partnered with Google and Academy Award-winning production company Framestore to produce a custom Google Cardboard headset and experience for iOS and Android.

The launch of the app coincided with the first appearance of the XC90 at the Los Angeles Auto Show.
