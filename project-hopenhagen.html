OVERVIEW

The “Hopenhagen” campaign was a global initiative created in support of the UN Climate Change Conference (COP15) in Copenhagen. Rather than framing climate change through fear, the campaign sought to inspire optimism and collective action. The film Together became one piece of that broader movement, helping rally more than six million supporters worldwide and demonstrating how creativity can shift public conversation at a global scale.
