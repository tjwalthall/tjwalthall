OVERVIEW

RaceTrac had been a staple for years, but they had never had a unified brand platform. Partnering with R/GA Austin, we developed Whatever Gets You Going — the retailer’s first-ever national brand campaign.

The strategy was to celebrate the chaotic, beautiful, and highly specific habits that fuel our daily lives. Whether it’s pizza for breakfast, coffee at 3 AM, or a frozen yogurt obsession, we positioned RaceTrac as the ultimate enabler of personal momentum.
