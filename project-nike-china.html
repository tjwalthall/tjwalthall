OVERVIEW

Translating “Just Do It.” into Chinese is a literal impossibility. The words do not exist. The brief was to bring the ethos of “Just Do It.” to life for a new generation of youth culture in mainland China.

We created an integrated platform led by The Next Wave anthem film, supported by a series of sub-films inspired by real athlete heroes who live the “Just Do It.” philosophy.
