OVERVIEW

For the Samsung Galaxy Note 4 launch, we were tasked with making complex technical features feel intuitive and engaging. At R/GA NY, we developed a series of “heightened demos”—short, narrative-driven digital films that moved beyond the typical dry product showcase.

We made technology the protagonist. The campaign scaled globally, with the “Photo War” spot alone generating over 3.2 million views on YouTube.
